Coffee's we use

Arabica

Arabica coffee is a species of coffee plant, scientifically known as Coffea arabica. It is the most widely cultivated species of coffee in the world and is known for its high-quality flavor and aroma.

Sherry

Sherry coffee is a type of flavored coffee that is infused with sherry, a fortified wine from the region of Jerez de la Frontera in southern Spain.

Robusta

Robusta coffee is another species of coffee plant, scientifically known as Coffea canephora. It is the second most widely cultivated species of coffee in the world, after Arabica.

ourService

Good service at a coffee bar is characterized by friendly and attentive staff who are knowledgeable about the menu and can provide recommendations to customers. They should be able to take orders quickly and accurately, and prepare drinks efficiently while maintaining high quality standards. The atmosphere of the coffee bar should be clean and comfortable, with a welcoming ambiance that encourages customers to linger and enjoy their drinks.
